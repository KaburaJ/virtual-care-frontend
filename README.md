## Overview

The Virtual Care is a comprehensive solution designed to support and empower pregnant individuals on their journey to a healthy and active pregnancy. This application aims to provide a personalized and safe fitness experience tailored to the unique needs and stages of pregnancy. By incorporating expert guidance, customizable workout plans, and health tracking features, our goal is to promote the well-being of expectant mothers throughout the entire maternity period.

## Intended Features

### 1. Personalized Fitness Plans

- **Customizable Workouts:** Tailor fitness plans based on individual preferences, fitness levels, and trimester-specific needs.
- **Variety of Exercises:** Access a diverse range of exercises suitable for different stages of pregnancy, including cardio, strength training, and prenatal yoga.

### 2. Expert Guidance

- **Professional Advice:** Receive expert guidance from certified prenatal fitness trainers and healthcare professionals.
- **Educational Content:** Access informative articles, videos, and resources covering pregnancy-related fitness, nutrition, and well-being.

### 3. Health Tracking

- **Pregnancy Milestones:** Track and celebrate key milestones during pregnancy.
- **Health Metrics:** Monitor and record vital health metrics such as weight gain, heart rate, and blood pressure.

### 4. Community Support

- **Connect with Other Moms:** Join a supportive community of expectant mothers to share experiences, tips, and advice.
- **Forum and Discussions:** Engage in discussions on various topics related to pregnancy, fitness, and maternal health.

### 5. Safety First

- **Medical Clearance Integration:** Facilitate secure sharing of fitness plans and progress with healthcare providers.
- **Safe Exercise Recommendations:** Receive real-time feedback on exercise safety based on the latest medical guidelines.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MSSQL Server
- **Authentication:** Redis Session Storage
- **Communication:** WebSocket for live share

## Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up and run the application locally:

1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/KaburaJ/virtual-care-frontend.git`
2. Navigate to the project directory: `cd virtual-care-frontend`
3. Install dependencies: `npm install`
4. Start the frontend and backend servers: `npm start`
